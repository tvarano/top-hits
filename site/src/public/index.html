Month In Review

A Personal Interest Spotify Tracker

Month in Review is a tool for Spotify to track users' interests in music. Whether over the course of a month or for the entire listening history of the user, anyone using Spotify can create a tailored playlist showing them their favorite songs.
Note: this tool will create playlists using your Spotify account.
